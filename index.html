About MJSBDLK

After getting my eigth League badge, I took on the Elite Four and absolutely annihilated them. While I love battling Pokémon, I also found that I really enjoyed putting together my own personal websites to whine about my problems.

As League Champion, I always make time for my fans, who love my unrelenting sarcasm and, I'm sure, appreciate my telling them that I will "smell them later."

I couldn't be happier as Pokémon League Champion. So long as my Rival, Red, doesn't show up to dethrone me, I anticipate being champion for life. That's pretty unlikely though, since he spends all of his time surfing up and down the east coast of Cinnabar Island, for some reason.

About My Name

Alright, let's address the elephant in the room. Yes, I was born to my late parents "Blue Oak," and yes, my name is now the significantly less appealing "MJSBDLK". While introducing me to my rival, Red, my boneheaded Gramps forgot my name, and for some comprehension-and-belief-beggaring reason, let Red name me. I've been MJSBDLK ever since.
